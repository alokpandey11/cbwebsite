The Era of Credit Card Sized Computer

  • Sarthak
  • IoT stuff
  • 14

The Raspberry Pi is a low cost, credit-card sized computer that plugs into a computer monitor or TV, and uses a standard keyboard and mouse. It is a capable little device that enables people of all ages to explore computing, and to learn how to program in languages like Scratch and Python.

Computers themselves, and software yet to be developed, will revolutionize the way we learn.

My favorite kind of pie- raspberry pi 🍓⁣⁣ ⁣⁣ Basically I am holding a credit-card-sized computer in my hand for 20$ 💳💻 Pretty cool, right? So what is so special about it and why to start loving it ?🤔 ⁣⁣⁣

💡The Idea. ⁣⁣⁣ ⁣⁣⁣ Initially it was designed as a microcomputer to teach children coding but the multi-functionality and coolness if it Escalated so quickly that now everyone from 0 to 99 years old using it 👨🏼‍🔧 The first model Pi Zero costed only 5$ awesome , I can buy a computer and a bottle of wine 🍷 What’s the deal with the name?⁣⁣⁣ ⁣⁣⁣ The “Raspberry” part is refering to the early years of “fruit naming boom” among companies such as @apple, 🍑Apricote and Acorn. “Pi” - as python, the only programming language it supposed to run. 🧐 The mystery solved🕵🏻‍♀⁣⁣⁣ ⁣⁣⁣

🔮The deal.⁣⁣⁣ ⁣⁣⁣ Okay, cool, you got some knowledge and can sound like a true geek in every company but what to do with all of this ? 🧠🙄⁣⁣⁣ ⁣⁣⁣ Hoho! 🤠 So what I am gonna do with my new computer? Play games! Lol 😂😂 . . Well.... Gonna explore it's pins and also get a new setup for it very soon (presently in shortage of money) ⁣ The next step is: #ai development 🤖!⁣ Also, Thanks to colaboratory from @google , who partnered with @raspberrypifoundation everyone can explore machine learning with microcomputer.⁣🙀 ⁣ So, I really encourage to grab a raspberry and try it out ! “Computer-under-budget” 👾

What’s more, the Raspberry Pi has the ability to interact with the outside world, and has been used in a wide array of digital maker projects, from music machines and parent detectors to weather stations and tweeting birdhouses with infra-red cameras. We want to see the Raspberry Pi being used by kids all over the world to learn to program and understand how computers work.
