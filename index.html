About codebugged

CODEBUGGED is an organisation or group of programmers including students and professionals who aim at Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning and Internet of Things based product development and we also run various workshops, seminars, webinars and other tutorials for students who wish to learn application-based programming. Besides, we also explore and innovate by applying programming in our daily lives and further promote our innovations on various platforms including social media.

This initiative was founded by Sarthak, an IoT and ML enthusiast and sophomore in Computer Science and Engineering.

In today’s era application-based programming is all what multinational companies are looking for in the students when the recruit them, which is also something which lacks in almost all of us due to a theory based syllabus. This is a small step through which computer science students and our future professionals can be enlightened about such technology at the right time.
